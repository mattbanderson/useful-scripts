Go to https://jira.di2e.net and click the "Create" button in the top navigation bar and populate the following information:

Project: Accounts (AC)
Issue Type: Add Me to Group
Group Select: PIR-ReadWrite
Justification: Support $PROJECT_NAME development

Check "Create another" and then click the "Create" button.

Submit a 2nd request with all the same information except this time the Group Select should be PIR-Stash-ReadWrite .

PIR-ReadWrite gets you access to JIRA and Confluence, PIR-Stash-ReadWrite is for BitBucket.

After creating each ticket, assign it to one of the approvers listed in the issue description of the ticket.
